Sysco - Screen Protector Warranty Registration Process 

 

Titanium Armor must be registered within 30 days of purchase in order to activate the coverage guarantee. 

 

1) Download the Warranty Life app from the Apple or Android app store 

2) Register the serial number written on the back of your warranty card labelled "Screen Repair Guarantee" and proof of purchase within the first 30 days of purchase. 

3) Ensure the warranty is registered to Sysco  

4) Follow the complete device diagnostics test to record the health of your smartphone
